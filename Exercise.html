The Best Movies according to Tawsif

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption

It is a compelling drama, and a very moving film, perfectly written, acted and shot. They just don't come much better than this.

The Conjuring

The 1970s setting is so well done, and the haunted house feels like a character in itself with all its creepy corners and eerie atmosphere. It draws you into the story and makes everything feel more real. Overall, The Conjuring is a fantastic horror movie that I'd recommend to anyone who loves a good scare.

Saw

James Wan's horror film Saw is full of demented twists and guilty-looking actors, but the scenario is cruel enough to make you squirm.
